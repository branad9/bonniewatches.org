Top 10 Perfect First Luxury Watches Under 10 lakh

Keith Brandie
View profile

Buying your first luxury watch can feel genuinely intimidating. The sheer number of brands, models, movements, sizes, and price points can overwhelm even the most enthusiastic newcomer. When you're spending several thousand pounds on a single watch, you want to be confident that you're making the right decision.

This guide focuses on safe, timeless, and proven luxury watches all under the 10 lakh price mark. These are watches from respected brands, with strong heritage, excellent build quality, and designs that won't look dated in ten, twenty, or even fifty years.

If you're buying your first luxury watch, chances are you're not looking at microbrands. You're looking at names like Omega, IWC, Grand Seiko, Tudor, Longines, Breitling, Rolex, and Christopher Ward. Let's begin.

1. Omega Aqua Terra (Black Lacquer Dial)

The Omega Aqua Terra is one of the safest first luxury watch choices on the market. The newest black lacquer dial version strips away the divisive teak pattern and replaces it with a clean, timeless aesthetic.

Available in 41mm, 38mm, and 34mm, the 38mm variant hits the sweet spot for most wrists. With 150m water resistance, sapphire crystal, and a METAS-certified Calibre 8800 movement, this is a true everyday luxury watch that can last a lifetime.

Price: ₹7,12,000

2. IWC Pilot's Watch Mark XX

The IWC Mark XX is the definition of versatility. It carries IWC's rich pilot heritage without the oversized, aggressive styling found in some aviation watches.

At 40mm wide and just 10.8mm thick, it wears beautifully. Inside is the Calibre 32111 automatic movement with an impressive 120-hour power reserve. It's understated, refined, and endlessly wearable.

Price: ₹6,50,000

3. Grand Seiko Shunbun (Spring Drive)

The Shunbun is a watch that converts enthusiasts into collectors. Crafted in high-intensity titanium, it's incredibly light on the wrist while maintaining exceptional durability.

The cherry-blossom inspired dial is breathtaking, and the Spring Drive Calibre 9R65 delivers near-quartz accuracy with mechanical soul. Swap it onto a NATO strap, and it transforms into a daily wearer.

Price: ₹5,20,000

4. Omega Speedmaster Professional (Sapphire Sandwich)

The Speedmaster needs no introduction. As one of the most historically significant watches ever made, it offers unmatched heritage.

The sapphire version provides everyday durability while showcasing the Calibre 3861 manual-wind movement. It's clean, purposeful, and eternally iconic.

Price: ₹8,59,100

5. Tudor Black Bay Monochrome

Tudor's Black Bay Monochrome is one of the best value propositions in luxury watches today. COSC and METAS-certified, 200m water resistance, and a rock-solid 70-hour power reserve.

The monochrome colourway ensures maximum versatility, making it ideal for a first luxury purchase.

Price: ₹4,67,300

6. Longines HydroConquest GMT

Longines consistently punches above its weight. The HydroConquest GMT offers serious specifications ceramic bezel, 300m water resistance, and a 72-hour power reserve at a remarkably accessible price.

Price: ₹3,16,000

7. Longines Conquest 38mm

Minimal, elegant, and endlessly wearable. The Conquest feels like a blend between an Omega Aqua Terra and a Rolex Explorer, yet it retains its own identity.

Price: ₹2,21,000

8. Breitling Top Time B31

Youthful, stylish, and modern. The Top Time B31 feels fresh while still rooted in Breitling's heritage. It's one of the few watches that genuinely shines on leather.

Price: ₹5,45,900

9. Rolex Explorer 36mm

The Rolex Explorer is simplicity perfected. At 36mm, it's comfortable, elegant, and suitable for every occasion imaginable.

Price: ₹7,35,500

10. Christopher Ward C60 Trident Pro 300

Christopher Ward offers exceptional quality at an accessible price. The C60 Trident Pro is robust, clean, and perfect for someone stepping into luxury watches without overspending.

Price: ₹1,12,950

Final Thoughts: Your first luxury watch should be something you feel comfortable wearing every single day. These ten watches are timeless, versatile, and respected the perfect foundation for any watch collection.
