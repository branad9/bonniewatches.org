Top 5 Audemars Piguet Super Clone Watches in India 2026 | Royal Oak & Code 11.59

Keith Brandie
View profile

Audemars Piguet is not just a watch brand — it is a statement of power, prestige, and elite taste. Established in 1875, the Swiss manufacturer has remained independent for over a century, producing some of the most technically complex and visually iconic watches in the world. Models like the Royal Oak, Royal Oak Offshore, and Code 11.59 are instantly recognizable and admired by collectors globally.

However, owning an original Audemars Piguet watch is a dream that very few can realistically fulfill. Authentic pieces often start above ₹30 lakh and can easily cross ₹1 crore depending on complications such as tourbillons, perpetual calendars, or skeletonized movements. Because of this, demand for Audemars Piguet super clone watches has grown massively in India.

Super clone watches are not ordinary replicas. They are premium 1:1 recreations built using high-grade materials, Swiss-style automatic movements, sapphire crystals, and precise finishing that visually matches the original watch up to 95–98%. For buyers who appreciate luxury aesthetics without paying absurd prices, AP first copy watches offer the perfect balance between design and affordability.

Audemars Piguet Royal Oak 15510ST Green Dial 41mm

The Royal Oak 15510ST is one of the most desired luxury sports watches ever produced. Designed originally by Gérald Genta, the Royal Oak broke every rule of traditional watchmaking by introducing stainless steel as a luxury material. The octagonal bezel with exposed screws has since become one of the most iconic designs in horological history.

The green Grande Tapisserie dial adds modern boldness while retaining classic AP DNA. In the super clone version, the dial texture, case thickness, and integrated bracelet closely match the original design. The automatic movement offers smooth sweeping seconds and reliable daily performance.

This model is extremely popular among buyers searching for a Royal Oak first copy in India, as it pairs effortlessly with both casual and premium outfits. The wrist presence is powerful yet elegant.

Royal Oak Flying Tourbillon 26530 Smoky Blue Dial

Tourbillon watches represent the highest level of mechanical craftsmanship. A flying tourbillon, in particular, removes the upper bridge, giving the illusion that the rotating cage is floating inside the dial. This complication is extremely expensive in original watches.

The smoky blue dial paired with the visible tourbillon opening creates an extraordinary visual effect. Super clone manufacturers have achieved remarkable accuracy in replicating the depth, color gradient, and movement visibility of this model.

This watch is ideal for buyers who want maximum luxury appearance and mechanical presence. It is often chosen by collectors who already own multiple watches and want something visually dominant.

Audemars Piguet Code 11.59 Selfwinding Black Dial

The Code 11.59 collection represents Audemars Piguet’s modern vision of dress watches. Unlike the sporty Royal Oak, this model focuses on elegance, curved lugs, and refined dial design.

The black dial version offers a minimalist yet premium aesthetic. The sapphire crystal creates a beautiful distortion at angles, a detail that is carefully replicated in high-end super clones.

This model is perfect for businessmen, formal occasions, and buyers who want a subtle luxury statement rather than an aggressive sports watch.

Royal Oak Perpetual Calendar Blue Dial 41mm

The perpetual calendar is one of the most admired complications in watchmaking. It automatically tracks day, date, month, and leap year, requiring no manual correction until the year 2100.

In the super clone version, the dial layout is replicated precisely to mirror the authentic Royal Oak. The blue Grande Tapisserie texture gives depth and elegance, making it one of the most visually balanced luxury watches available.

This watch appeals strongly to collectors who appreciate mechanical complexity combined with Royal Oak heritage.

Code 11.59 Tourbillon Meteorite Dial 41mm

Meteorite dials are among the rarest dial materials in the luxury watch industry. Each meteorite pattern is naturally unique, meaning no two watches ever look the same.

Combined with a tourbillon complication, this watch sits at the top tier of visual luxury. The super clone version delivers extraordinary presence, making it one of the most premium-looking AP replicas available today.

This model is ideal for buyers who want something extremely rare, bold, and conversation-starting.
